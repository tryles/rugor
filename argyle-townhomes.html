Argyle Townhomes

Driving the sales process for a townhome development in the Lower Mainland.

A site that showcases a Lower Mainland townhome development with floor plans, an interactive neighbourhood map and warranty protection information.

The landing page features a full-page image slider

which leads into the neighbourhood map and floorplans.

Each plan can be navigated-into through the interactive site plan and the sub-menu. The site plan scales across screen sizes and devices.

And each unit plan features its own unique url for SEO and quick reference. Clicking expands the plan to full-screen.

The team page communicates details about the builder, the sales people, and home warranty.

The registration page captures sales leads with and integrates to a third-party service.

Conclusion

Simple and organically-grown, the Argyle Townhomes site communicates what is necessary to without going over-the-top. Finishing touches like the interactive community map and site plan help to keep the site personal and connected to potential buyers.
