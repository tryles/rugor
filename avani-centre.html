Avani Centre

Avani Centre is a 30-storey development situated at the southern gateway to Surrey City Centre.

A unique residential tower and a 5-storey Hampton by Hilton hotel, Avani Centre brings world-class comfort and style to everyday living. The site features studio residences to three-bedroom floor plans, and 8 two-level cityhomes.

The site provides interactive floor and site plans while communicating the lifestyle one can expect living at Avani Centre.

Integrated residential and hotel amenities are highlighted.

And the benefits of the surrounding neighbourhood are expressed in an interactive neighbourhood map. The map is repsonsive and interactive on both desktop and mobile.

The floor plans are navigable by a sub-menu as well as by interactive floor plate.

And the plans themselves expand into larger views and direct download. The panorama viewer defaults the highest floor for each plan and can be navigated via secondary navigation.

All pages and features of the site are optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile to provide a consistent browsing experience. The registration form features real-time formatting to normalize data for an external lead-generation service.

Conclusion

An effective website and tool to facilitate sales of a 30-storey residential tower.
