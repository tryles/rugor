Avani Investment Group

An online presence for a property developer focused on hospitality.

Avani Investment Group needed a site to showcase their property development portfolio as well as communicate their core values.

Taking queues from their flagship development Avani Centre, the corporate site provides a company history and establishes the partnerships which have contributed to their success.

The about page speaks to the company's core values.

While the portfolio categorizes development types.

The contact page has an interactive map and form with field-validation.

The site also features a news feed for articles about the company's interests and development announcements.

Conclusion

A corporate site is an effective way to support the credibilty and sales of future real-estate developments. By showcasing partnerships and clearly communicating core values, the company is perceived as grounded and down-to-earth.
