Mountain Creations

An online presence for an established home-builder on Vancouver's North Shore.

A fresh look for a B.C. home builder who takes pride in a commitment to craftsmanship and responsible building practices.

Mountain Creations wanted to communicate their company mission, showcase their recent and upcoming developments, and provide resources for new and returning home-buyers.

The home page serves as an introduction to the company and their current projects.

While the past projects page highlights their home-building history across the lower mainland.

The news feed links to individual news articles with sharing buttons for numerous social media platforms.

And the contact page features a custom location map with a contact form that links into a lead-generation workflow.

Additionally, the resources page features a live mortgage calculator as well as tips for first-time home buyers.

Conclusion

Lightweight and clean, the site design allows the user focus on the details that will ultimately help them reach a buying decision. Along the way, the builder's story comes across in a clear way while communicating the company's core values.
